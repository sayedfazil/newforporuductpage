wholesale buying guide

The Microsoft 13.5" Multi-Touch Surface Laptop 5 for Business is a high-performance laptop designed specifically for professional use. Featuring a sleek matte black metal exterior, it offers a sophisticated and professional look.

Running on the latest Windows 11 Pro operating system, this laptop is equipped with an Intel Core i7-1265U (12th Gen) processor. With a dual-core configuration and a maximum clock speed of 4.8 GHz, it delivers powerful and efficient performance. The 8-core efficiency CPU offers a clock speed of up to 3.6 GHz, ensuring smooth multitasking and improved overall efficiency.

The laptop comes with 32 GB of LPDDR5x memory, providing ample space for running multiple applications simultaneously. The 12 MB L3 cache further enhances the laptop's speed and responsiveness.

Featuring integrated Intel Iris Xe graphics, the laptop delivers stunning visuals and smooth graphics performance. The 13.5" LCD panel offers a 3:2 aspect ratio and a resolution of 2256 x 1504 pixels, providing sharp and vibrant visuals. The touchscreen feature adds convenience and versatility to the user experience.

With a total installed storage capacity of 512 GB in the form of a 512 GB M.2 NVMe PCIe solid-state drive, the laptop provides ample space for storing files, documents, and applications. The absence of an optical drive makes the laptop even slimmer and more lightweight.

In terms of connectivity, the laptop offers a USB-A 3.0/3.1/3.2 Gen 1 port and a Thunderbolt 4 USB-C port that supports DisplayPort and power delivery. It also includes a 3.5 mm headphone output, two far-field microphones, and built-in speakers. Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) with MU-MIMO support and Bluetooth 5.2 ensure fast and reliable wireless connectivity.

The laptop features a user-facing 720p webcam for video conferencing and virtual meetings. With an estimated battery life of up to 18 hours, it provides long-lasting productivity on the go.

Additional features include a notebook keyboard with backlight for easy typing in low-light environments, a touchpad for smooth navigation, and dedicated hardware TPM security chip and facial recognition for enhanced security.

With dimensions of 12.1 x 8.8 x 0.6" and a weight of 2.86 lb, this laptop is lightweight and portable, making it ideal for business professionals who are always on the go. The package weight is 4.74 lb, and the box dimensions are 15.6 x 11.7 x 2.1".

Overall, the Microsoft 13.5" Multi-Touch Surface Laptop 5 for Business offers exceptional performance, a sleek design, and a range of features that make it a suitable choice for professionals in need of a reliable and efficient laptop.
